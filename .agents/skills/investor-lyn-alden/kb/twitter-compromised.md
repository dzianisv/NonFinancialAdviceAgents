# Twitter/X Restored

*My Twitter/X account was compromised from February 26th to March 3rd, but I confirm that I've regained control of it now.
Thank you to everyone who helped make it happen.
Fortunately, I still had access to Nostr during that time (e.g. primal.net/lyn), which is decentralized social media.
Eight months ago, at the Oslo Freedom Forum, I had a fireside chat with Twitter founder Jack Dorsey about the power of Nostr and decentralized social media in general. He has been a big contributor and advocate for this type of technology, including Nostr specifically.
