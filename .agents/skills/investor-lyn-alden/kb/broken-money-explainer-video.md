# Broken Money Explainer Video

*In my book *Broken Money*, I wrote about the past, present, and future of money through the lens of technology. And importantly, I put a lot of focus on connecting some of those technological or quantitative elements to the human impact that they have had.
I have now written, narrated, and produced a 30-minute explainer video that covers some of the main themes of the book. It walks the viewer through the history of money and banking, describes the major problems that they have run into over time, and discusses what some of the emerging technological trends may offer in terms of future improvement. And with the help of the Tranches graphics team, it comes alive with animation that helps visualize the key concepts to accompany the narration.
